# FLAGGED

Flagged is a simple tool centered around dating that helps people understand the tone and behavior inside a conversation.

Users paste a text conversation and instantly see whether there are communication red flags.

The goal of Flagged is to answer one question clearly:

"Is this a red flag?"

The product should feel human, clear, and emotionally understandable.  
It should never feel like a complicated AI report.

Results should be easy to understand and easy to share.

---

## Core Result

Every conversation receives a verdict:

GREEN  
The conversation appears healthy.

CAUTION  
Some concerning patterns appear.

FLAGGED  
Strong red flag behavior detected.

The verdict should feel intuitive and human, not robotic.

---

## Premium Analysis

A premium version of Flagged provides deeper insights including:

- explanation of detected behavior
- pattern breakdown (gaslighting, manipulation, insults, etc.)
- relationship communication insights
- suggestions on what to watch for next

The premium analysis should feel thoughtful and helpful, not overly technical.

---

## Product Principles

Flagged should always prioritize:

1. clarity
2. emotional understanding
3. human language
4. shareability
5. speed

A user should be able to understand the result within seconds.
